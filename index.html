About Me

Hey there! I'm Yaryna, a 21-year-old Ukrainian girl who likes pink (as you can probably already tell). I'm currently a student at LCC International University in Lithuania, but that's just one of the three of my study abroad experiences.

I've always had a passion for traveling and exploring different cultures. In 2019-2020, I spent a year at McIntosh High School in Georgia, USA, during my junior year of high school. Later, I went to Kyung Hee University in South Korea for a semester during my sophomore year.

My love for international experiences and my own challenges of fitting into new communities inspired me to create this website. Here, I will tell about my experiences, provide tips for students who plan their own exchange, and share exciting opportunities.

Welcome to the Pink Odyssey where we will delv into diverse cultures and make the most out of it together!
